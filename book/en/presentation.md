# Author's Presentation

**Dr. Álvaro Lozano Murciego** is a Spanish academic and researcher specializing in the field of Computer Engineering.

## Academic and Professional Profile
*   **Current position:** Associate Professor (Profesor Titular) at the **University of Salamanca (USAL)**, within the Department of Computer Science and Automation.
*   **Education:** PhD in Computer Engineering from the University of Salamanca (2019). His doctoral thesis was titled *"Vehicle route optimization system with stochastic demands including predictive models"*. He also holds a Master's in Intelligent Systems and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the same institution.
*   **Research groups:** He is part of several research groups, including **ESALAB** (Expert Systems and Applications Laboratory), **GTC** (Artificial Intelligence, Sensor Networks, Security and Advanced Computing), and **MIDA** (Data Mining).

## Research Interests
His work focuses on various areas of computing, notably:
*   Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
*   Recommendation Systems.
*   Natural Language Processing (NLP), Internet of Things (IoT), Edge Computing, Android application development, and route optimization.

In addition to his research, he has worked as an Android developer and holds certifications in that field.
